Leadership Framework

Authenticity

Serves as a role model for moral and fair behavior. A transparent approach commands esteem and confidence from team members.

Coaching

Acts as a mentor who supports employee growth. Team members are nurtured towards achieving their highest levels of performance.

Shares purpose and hopeful vision for colleagues to follow. Team members are compelled and inspired to exceed goals.

Focuses on taking risks and spurring ingenuity. Knowledge is valued and challenges are viewed as learning opportunities.
