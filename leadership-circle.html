Understanding the Framework

The Leadership Circle Profile organizes leadership competencies into two primary domains: Creative competencies (top half) represent leadership behaviors that lead to high fulfillment and effectiveness. Reactive tendencies (bottom half) reflect leadership behaviors that limit effectiveness and authentic expression.

The assessment measures across two key axes: Task (achieving results) and Relationship (fostering connections), providing insight into leadership balance and style.
